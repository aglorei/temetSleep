About

My name is Tien, and I'd love to take a whack at your problems.

I grew up without cable or videogames, and had to learn to be creative in how I entertain myself. To the dismay of my parents, I took apart my first radio because there was nothing else to do after school that day. The thrill of trying to understand what was happening underneath, coupled with the excitement of figuring out how to put it back together again (in possibly better ways) meant that I developed a passion for problem-solving at an early age. As I grew older, radios and TVs became DNA and chromosomes and mathematical laws and chemical reactions. Shortly after moving to the Pacific Northwest in late 2014, my focus transitioned to code.
